Reasons I Have to Stay Alive

In no particular order of importance... just numbered in order of how they came to mind

  1. Family, of course
  2. My stupid loud little dog
  3. My orange cat who hits me
  4. The softness of moth wings
  5. David Bowie as Jareth from the Labyrinth
  6. My pet serpents, who do not actually know me nor understand the concept of love
  7. Warm, muggy summer nights lit by fireflies and dim street lamps
  8. Dragons, in all their majesty
  9. Those round pastries from the supermarket with cold cream in the center
  10. Pure, unadulterated spite and hatred for others
  11. Banana milkshakes
  12. Anthropology, and the general amazing history of humanity
  13. I think some people would miss me
  14. Men in tight-fitting clothes
  15. The wonderful complexities of bugs, much of which is still yet undiscovered
  16. Eventually meeting alien life?
  17. Young Axl Rose
  18. Garrett from camp, whose personality and life views left an important impression on me last summer
  19. Avoid becoming part of the 41%, which only proves the haters right
  20. I don't want to think about my parents crying
  21. Pulp Fiction
