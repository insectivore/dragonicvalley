The process of accepting the hard truth that neither my family nor my closest friends genuinely give a shit about any of my projects or creative efforts has been a painful one. As a person who, in childhood, was constantly showered with praise and encouragement by the adults around me for my mediocre artistic ventures, it's a shocking and hollow feeling to realize that neither your parents nor your best friend genuinely care about the things you put time into creating - like this website, for example.

However, I'm trying to look on the bright side of things.
